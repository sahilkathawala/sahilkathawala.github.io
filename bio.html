about me

pocket photographer

I don't consider myself a photographer, and I don't know much about cameras or photocomposition. My dad, however, is a serial photographer, having photo-documented much of my life, and he's interest in preserving life's little moments has definitely rubbed off on me. This year I decided I'd start taking more time to document those small moments and started compiling photos I take in a picture blog. I've been using VSCO Cam to take and edit photos on my phone (if you don't already use it, I highly recommend it, it's amazing) and uploading them onto a VSCO grid, which organizes them into a really beautiful layout. Subjects mostly just include my friends and places I've been. Hopefully you find them as interesting as I do.
